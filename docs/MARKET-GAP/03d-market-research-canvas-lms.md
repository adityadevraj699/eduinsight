# Market Research — Canvas LMS (Feedback/Surveys)

## Kya hai
Instructure ka Canvas LMS — global top LMS (US/Europe/India ke premium private universities me popular). Feedback/surveys ek feature hai, poora product nahi.

## Feature Findings
| Area | Finding |
|---|---|
| Data collection | Quizzes/Surveys tool ke through rating + text responses collect hote hain |
| Analysis | Basic quiz statistics — question-wise response distribution |
| Sentiment analysis | ❌ Core Canvas me nahi. (Third-party integrations jaise Qualtrics/Explorance Blue via LTI plugin hi AI layer laate hain) |
| Roles/RBAC | Canvas ka role system LMS-wide hai (Admin/Teacher/Student), feedback-specific HOD role concept nahi |
| Multi-tenant | Canvas khud SaaS multi-tenant hai (per-institution), but feedback-intelligence multi-university comparison nahi deta |
| Notifications | Standard LMS notification system — course announcements ke jaisa, "AI report ready" wala use case nahi |
| Cost | Heavy — enterprise LMS licensing (per-student pricing), chhoti colleges ke liye costly |
| Focus | Poora LMS hai (courses, assignments, grading) — feedback iska ek chhota corner hai, primary product nahi |

## Kya missing hai (Gap)
1. Canvas "feedback-first" product nahi hai — feedback sirf ek side-feature hai.
2. AI insight ke liye third-party paid add-on chahiye (Qualtrics/Explorance jaisa), jo cost aur complexity dono badhata hai.
3. Chhoti/mid-size Indian colleges ke liye Canvas pricing hi barrier hai — wo isse feedback ke liye kabhi use nahi karenge, sirf LMS ke liye rakhte hain agar afford kar sakein.

## EduInsight ke liye Takeaway
Canvas heavy aur costly hai, primarily LMS hai feedback-tool nahi. EduInsight ek focused, lightweight, feedback-intelligence-only product ban sakta hai jo chhoti/mid colleges afford kar sakein — jo Canvas ka scope kabhi nahi tha.
