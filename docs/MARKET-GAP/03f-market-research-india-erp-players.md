# Market Research — India-specific College ERP / NAAC-NBA Players (Local Context — bahut important for aapka target market)

Agar aap India me colleges target kar rahe ho, to real competition ye "College ERP" companies hain — kyunki feedback collection India me largely **NAAC/NBA accreditation compliance** se driven hota hai, sirf teaching-improvement se nahi.

## Key Finding: NAAC/NBA context
- NAAC Criterion I & II me explicitly "feedback on curriculum from students/employers" aur "structured feedback on teaching quality" chahiye hota hai as documentary evidence.
- Isliye Indian colleges feedback isliye collect karte hain taaki accreditation ke time SSR/AQAR report me evidence dikha sakein — sirf "faculty improvement" ke liye nahi.

## Players in this space
| Player | Feedback Handling | AI/Sentiment | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| Camu (octoze) | "Structured feedback forms with computed analysis" — part of full ERP | ❌ Not AI, just tabulation | NAAC/NBA/NIRF full accreditation suite |
| EduSys | Feedback ek module hai poore college ERP (admission, fees, library) ke andar | ❌ | General college management |
| LearnQoch | Automated mapping of uploaded feedback data to NAAC/NBA/NIRF criteria | ❌ | Accreditation report generation |
| Kalvisalai NAAC Suite | SSR/IQAC/CO-PO/feedback sab ek jagah | ❌ | Full NAAC documentation suite |
| Edhitch | SAR/SSR reporting, CO-PO attainment tracking | ❌ | Accreditation + academic ops |

## Kya missing hai in sab (Gap)
1. Feedback yahan **compliance-checkbox** hai — koi bhi in tools me AI sentiment/theme/summary analysis nahi milta.
2. Ye sab full ERP suites hain — feedback ek chhota module hai, standalone "feedback intelligence" product koi nahi banaya.
3. Faculty ko actionable insight nahi milta — data bas accreditation report ke liye "evidence" banta hai, teaching improve karne ke liye nahi.

## EduInsight ke liye Takeaway (Strategic)
Ye India ka **sabse bada real gap** hai:
> Indian ERP players feedback ko "accreditation paperwork" ki tarah treat karte hain. Koi bhi unme se AI-powered sentiment/theme insight nahi deta jo faculty ko actually improve karne me help kare.

Agar EduInsight ye do cheezein combine kar de:
- (a) NAAC/NBA-ready structured feedback + evidence export (jo Indian ERPs karte hain)
- (b) AI sentiment/theme/actionable-insight layer (jo sirf expensive Western tools karte hain)

...to ye ek clear, defensible, **India-specific white space** ban jaata hai jahan koi single competitor abhi nahi hai.
