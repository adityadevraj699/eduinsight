# Market Research — AI-Native Competitors (⚠️ Important — inke baare me original problem statement me mention nahi tha)

Aapke problem statement me sirf 4 tools compare kiye gaye the (Google Forms, MS Forms, Moodle, Canvas) — but ye sab "dumb form" category ke hain. Reality me EduInsight ka **asli competition** wo tools hain jo already AI + sentiment analysis kar rahe hain higher-ed feedback ke liye. Ye research isliye critical hai.

## 1. Explorance Blue
- Purpose-built course-evaluation platform, end-to-end automation (invites, reminders, reporting).
- AI-powered sentiment/qualitative analysis + "harmful language auto-redaction" for faculty psychological safety.
- LMS integration: Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle.
- Weakness: Standardized rating-form model, response rates often <30%; setup/administration complex; expensive enterprise contracts.

## 2. Watermark Course Evaluations & Surveys
- 900+ institutions use it. AI-powered summaries, sentiment analysis, "guided insights and recommended actions."
- Connects to Watermark Faculty Success (ties feedback into faculty performance reviews) and accreditation workflow.
- Weakness: Enterprise/mid-market pricing, requires separate tools for non-evaluation surveys, heavy for smaller colleges.

## 3. Qualtrics (Course Evaluation module)
- General XM platform repurposed for course evals — powerful survey logic + AI sentiment scoring.
- 8,800+ schools trust it, but **Qualtrics removed its discounted Academic Plan**, pushing costs much higher — many universities are actively looking for alternatives now.
- Weakness: Not purpose-built for SET (Student Evaluation of Teaching); still fundamentally a static form with AI bolted on.

## 4. Perspective AI (emerging 2026 category leader)
- Different approach entirely — replaces static rating-scale forms with an **AI interviewer** that asks follow-up questions in real time, capturing the "why" behind a score, not just the number.
- Positioned as the response-rate + depth solution that static-form + AI-sentiment tools (Qualtrics, Explorance) can't match.

## 5. Student Voice AI
- UK-based, institution-wide open-text feedback analysis; sentence-level theme classification, benchmarked reporting.
- Used by universities like Birmingham City University.

## Common Pattern Across All of These
- Sab already "form + AI sentiment" model kar chuke hain — is idea me EduInsight first-mover nahi hai globally.
- Sab **enterprise-priced**, Western-university-focused, English-heavy universities ke liye design kiye gaye hain.
- Koi bhi **India-specific** nahi hai — NAAC/NBA/AICTE accreditation workflows, Hindi/regional language support, ya budget-friendly per-student pricing kisi me nahi mila.
- Perspective AI ka "conversational/follow-up" model dikhata hai ki sirf sentiment-on-top-of-form bhi ab "table stakes" ban raha hai, differentiation ab depth aur action-loop me hai.

## EduInsight ke liye Takeaway (Important)
Apna problem statement update karo — "no AI competitor exists" wala framing galat hai. Sahi framing ye honi chahiye:
> "AI-powered course feedback analysis already exists globally (Explorance, Watermark, Qualtrics, Perspective AI), but ye sab expensive, enterprise-only, aur Indian accreditation (NAAC/NBA) context ke liye design nahi kiye gaye. EduInsight ka gap yahi hai — affordable, India-context-aware, accreditation-linked AI feedback intelligence for mid-size colleges."
